PROJECT OVERVIEW
This project analyzes regulatory quality in Japan and Russia from 2015 to 2021.
Regulatory quality refers to the ability of governments to design and implement
policies and regulations that support private sector development, economic stability,
and investor confidence.

OBJECTIVES
1. To study the concept of regulatory quality
2. To compare regulatory systems of Japan and Russia
3. To analyze regulatory quality trends (2015–2021)
4. To examine policy reforms using case studies
5. To suggest improvements in regulatory governance

- Secondary data analysis
- Comparative approach
- Time-series analysis (2015–2021)

KEY FINDINGS
Japan shows consistently high regulatory quality due to transparency and
strong institutional frameworks. Russia exhibits fluctuating regulatory
quality due to sanctions, regulatory uncertainty, and enforcement issues.

CONCLUSION
Effective regulatory frameworks are essential for economic growth and
investor confidence. Japan maintains regulatory stability, while Russia
requires reforms to improve transparency and predictability.

PROJECT OVERVIEW
This assignment analyzes state-wise road infrastructure development in
Central India using data on National Highways (NH), State Highways (SH),
and total road length between 2016 and 2019.

The study focuses on Madhya Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Jharkhand, and
West Bengal to understand infrastructural disparities and policy needs.

OBJECTIVE
- To examine state-wise road infrastructure development
- To identify trends and disparities across states
- To support policy formulation for balanced regional development

DATA USED
- State-wise Length of National Highways (2016–2019)
- State-wise Length of State Highways (2016–2019)
- State-wise Total Road Length (2016–2019)

METHODOLOGY
- Secondary data collection from government and published sources
- Descriptive statistical analysis
- Trend comparison using tables and graphs

KEY FINDINGS
- Madhya Pradesh shows the highest growth in road infrastructure
- Jharkhand and Chhattisgarh show steady improvement
- West Bengal shows fluctuations in highway expansion

POLICY CONTEXT

Pariyojana, PMGSY, PPP models, and Smart City Mission.
